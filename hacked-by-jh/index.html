Hacked by JH

Hacked, the dynamic fusion of technological enthusiasm and collaborative learning, intricately designed by students for students. With a vibrant community of over 850+ members, Hacked serves as an energetic platform where passion converges with expertise across seven domains, including web and app development, AI/ML, cybersecurity, web 3, DSA, and prototype and robotics.


The founders of Hacked had a very clear vision: “To create a space where students passionate about technology can come together to learn, collaborate, and apply their skills to real life challenges”. What distinguishes Hacked is its unwavering commitment to inclusivity, offering free services to all. Fueled by industry leaders such as Google and AWS, our events, workshops, and hackathons cultivate an environment where hands-on experiences thrive. We at Hacked have pledged to Learn, Innovate and grow. Hacked is more than a club; it's a transformative journey where innovation finds its space.

FAQs


Hacked is a dynamic fusion of technological enthusiasm and collaborative learning designed by students for students. With a vibrant community of over 850+ members, it serves as an energetic platform for expertise across various domains. Anyone passionate about technology and collaborative learning is welcome to join the community.

Hacked organizes a diverse array of workshops, hackathons, and events. These activities are designed to provide hands-on experiences and are open to all members of the community.

Hacked is committed to inclusivity, offering free services to all members and ensuring that everyone, regardless of their background or financial constraints, can participate in all the events and activities.

Hacked covers Eight core domains:
Web and App development
AI/ML
Cybersecurity
Web3
Data Science
DSA
Prototype and Robotics.
These domains provide tailored learning experiences and cater to varying levels of expertise and interest.

Members of Hacked benefit from mentorship by industry professionals, hands-on learning experiences, and the opportunity to apply their skills to real-world challenges. It's also a space to cultivate practical competencies and engage with a community of like-minded individuals.
